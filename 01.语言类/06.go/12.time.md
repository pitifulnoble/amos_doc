# go时间

## 1.解析

```go
timeT, _ := time.Parse("2006-01-02", "2020-04-14")
fmt.Println(timeT)

timeT, _ = time.Parse("2006-01-02 15:04:05", "2020-04-14 15:00:00")
fmt.Println(timeT)

timeT, _ = time.Parse("06-01-02", "20-04-14")
fmt.Println(timeT)

timeT, _ = time.Parse("2006-Jan-02", "2020-Apr-14")
fmt.Println(timeT)
```
